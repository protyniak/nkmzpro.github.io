Story about us

“Novokramatorsky mashinostroitelny zavod” is the largest heavy engineering enterprise of tailor-made production in Europe. The company has been successfully operating for 85 years in the market of metallurgical, mining, press-forging, sinking and tunneling, handling and specialized equipment. The rich history of the company includes a lot of achievements marked as “created for the first time ever”, including items for the space industry. NKMZ is always open for cooperation.
